# Specific Affiliate Programs to Apply To (2026)

Focus first on the highest-trust, highest-converting sites on your list. These are real programs that still pay.

## Tier 1 – Highest Priority (Apply Immediately)

### 1. Swagbucks (Prodege)
- **Network**: Impact, FlexOffers, Admitad, direct Prodege
- **Typical payout**: ~$1.50–$3.00+ per verified registration / email confirm
- **Cookie**: Usually 30 days
- **Why**: Highest quality traffic, strong brand, easy conversion on “reliable daily earnings” angle
- **Apply**: https://www.prodege.com/affiliate-programs/ or search “Swagbucks” on Impact / FlexOffers

### 2. InboxDollars
- **Network**: Impact, MaxBounty, VigLink, Prodege family
- **Typical payout**: $2–$3 CPA + residual (often 10–30% of referred user’s earnings for life)
- **Why**: Cash (not just gift cards) appeals strongly; residual income is rare and powerful
- **Apply**: Impact campaign or MaxBounty offers; also check Prodege

### 3. Publishers Clearing House (PCH)
- **Network**: FlexOffers, Sovrn, older Impact campaigns
- **Payout**: Varies (often CPA on membership or high-intent leads)
- **Why**: Massive brand recognition and trust
- **Note**: Be careful with creative – never imply guaranteed wins
- **Apply**: Search FlexOffers / Sovrn for “Publishers Clearing House”

## Tier 2 – Strong Secondary

### 4. Prizegrab / similar daily cash portals
- Search FlexOffers, MaxBounty, OfferVault, or Contact the site directly for a partner program
- Many Globalizer-style sites run CPA campaigns ($1–$4 per lead)

### 5. Free Sample Platforms
- **PINCHme**: Often has influencer / referral style (commission on qualified users). Check their partner or “for creators” page.
- **SampleSource**: Look for affiliate / partner links
- **Influenster / BzzAgent style programs**: Good for sample-focused content

### 6. General Rewards Networks
- **Prodege** (owns Swagbucks, MyPoints, InboxDollars, etc.) – one application can unlock multiple brands
- **MaxBounty** – lots of survey / DOI / freebie offers
- **FlexOffers** – huge catalog of coupon + rewards + sweepstakes merchants
- **Impact.com** – premium brands including many rewards programs

## How to Apply Successfully
1. Use a professional email + website URL (your Cloudflare Pages or custom domain)
2. In the application notes write something like:
   > “I run a high-trust sweepstakes & free-samples comparison site that ranks offers by legitimacy and real winner proof. Traffic is highly targeted freebie/sweepstakes seekers. Looking for clean, disclosed partnerships only.”
3. Have a clear affiliate disclosure already on the site
4. Start with 2–3 applications, get approved, then expand

## Tracking & Best Practices
- Use your own short links or a free tool (Pretty Links, ThirstyAffiliates, or Cloudflare Workers for redirects)
- Always add `rel="sponsored"` or `rel="nofollow sponsored"` on affiliate links
- Keep a simple spreadsheet: Site | Network | Offer ID | Payout | Status | Notes
- Re-check offers every 30–60 days – rates and availability change

## What to Avoid
- High-spam CPA networks that push “you won $750 gift card” quiz funnels
- Any program that requires you to use misleading claims
- Promoting sites you scored 7+ on spammy-ness just for the commission
